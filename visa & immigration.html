Requirement for Visa Application

  1. Visa Application Form: Complete the appropriate visa application form for your chosen country.
  2. Valid Passport: A current passport with at least six months validity beyond your intended stay.
  3. Passport-Sized Photographs: Recent photographs that meet the specific requirements of the country.
  4. Proof of Financial Capacity: Evidence of sufficient funds to cover tuition fees and living expenses.
  5. Proof of Accommodation: Details about where you will be staying during your visit.
  6. Health and Character Checks: You may need to undergo a health examination and provide police clearance certificates.
  7. Visa Application Fee: Payment for the visa application, which varies by country and visa type.
